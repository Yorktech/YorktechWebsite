    WELCOME TO YORKTECH SOLUTIONS

    We're a software company based in York (uk). Specialising in Systems Integration, complex web applications, and mobile development.

    We specialise in Systems Integration. We have over 15 years experience of 'gluing' systems together and making them talk to each other.

    Some examples include Flight operations systems, Banking, E-commerce and public sector systems.

    We've provided software development consultancy services to business's of all sizes and both local and national public sector bodies.

    We've worked in a wide range of industries including Banking, Defence, Travel and Aviation, E-commerce / Billing Systems and the Public sector.

    We've recently worked with a local public sector body to improve their online services provision and to integrate these with their back end systems.

    Our other most recent projects have included development work on City of York Councils Oracle CRM, BreakingCharts.com, Jet2.com's e-commerce website, a flight planning systems integration project for Jet2.com and an E-commerce system for a major fancy dress retailer.
